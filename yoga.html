✨ Benefits of Yoga for Diabetes

📉 Lower Blood Sugar

Regular yoga practice can help improve insulin sensitivity and glucose uptake by muscles.

😌 Reduce Stress

Breathing exercises and meditation help lower cortisol levels, which can affect blood sugar.

💪 Build Strength

Yoga poses help build lean muscle mass, which improves metabolism and glucose control.

🧠 Mental Clarity

Mindfulness practices improve focus and help manage the emotional aspects of diabetes.

❤️ Heart Health

Yoga can help lower blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health.

😴 Better Sleep

Relaxation techniques promote better sleep quality, important for blood sugar regulation.

⚠️ Tips for Practicing Yoga with Diabetes

  • Check your blood sugar before and after your yoga session
  • Keep fast-acting glucose nearby in case of low blood sugar
  • Stay hydrated throughout your practice
  • Start slowly and gradually increase intensity
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed
  • Consult your doctor before starting a new exercise routine
  • Practice at the same time each day for consistent results

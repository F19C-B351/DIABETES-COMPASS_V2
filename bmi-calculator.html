Understanding BMI for Diabetes Management

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 and above

Diabetes & Weight

Maintaining a healthy weight is crucial for diabetes management. Even a small weight loss of 5-10% can significantly improve blood sugar control and reduce the risk of diabetes complications.

Healthy Goals

Work with your healthcare provider to set realistic weight and calorie goals. Gradual, sustainable changes in diet and physical activity are most effective for long-term diabetes management.

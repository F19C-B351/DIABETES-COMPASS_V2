Privacy Policy

🛡️ GDPR Compliant

1. Introduction

Diabetes Compass ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you submit your story through our "Share Your Story" feature, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Controller

Diabetes Compass is the data controller for the personal data collected through this form. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at: denirylands@gmail.com

3. Personal Data We Collect

When you submit your story, we collect:

  • Email Address (required) - Used to contact you regarding your submission
  • Name and Surname (optional) - Used for attribution if your story is published
  • Story Content - Your personal experience you choose to share
  • Selected Topic - The category of your story

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you provide by checking the consent checkbox and submitting the form.

5. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data will be used to:

  • Review and potentially publish your story on our website to inspire others in the diabetes community
  • Contact you regarding your submission or for clarification
  • Respond to any questions or concerns you may have

6. Data Sharing

We will never:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your email address publicly
  • Use your data for marketing without separate consent

If your story is published, only your name (if provided) and story content will be visible. Anonymous submissions will be published without attribution.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or until you request deletion. Published stories may remain on our website unless you request their removal.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure - Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing - Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability - Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent - Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint - File a complaint with a supervisory authority

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

10. Contact Us

To exercise any of your rights or for questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: denirylands@gmail.com

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

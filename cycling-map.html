🗺️ Cycling Routes for Diabetics

Explore safe, scenic cycling routes in Sofia and beyond. These routes are selected with diabetics in mind—featuring rest stops, accessible locations, and varying difficulty levels. Always carry your glucose supplies and ride safely!

📍 Sofia Cycling Map

🚴 Popular Cycling Routes

South Park (Yuzhen Park)

📏 5-8 km loop ⏱️ 30-45 min Easy

Flat, paved cycling paths through Sofia's largest park. Multiple rest areas, water fountains, and cafes. Perfect for beginners.

View on Map →

Borisova Gradina (Boris's Garden)

📏 6 km loop ⏱️ 35-50 min Easy

Historic park with shaded cycling lanes. Beautiful scenery, gentle terrain, and many benches for rest stops.

View on Map →

Vitosha Boulevard to NDK

📏 3 km one-way ⏱️ 15-20 min Easy

Urban cycling through Sofia's main pedestrian street. Wide paths, plenty of cafes, and access to South Park.

View on Map →

Pancharevo Lake Loop

📏 15 km loop ⏱️ 1-1.5 hours Moderate

Scenic route around the beautiful Pancharevo Lake. Some gentle hills, great views, restaurants at the lake.

View on Map →

Iskar River Trail

📏 20 km ⏱️ 1.5-2 hours Moderate

Follow the Iskar River through scenic landscapes. Mix of paved and gravel paths, multiple village stops for snacks.

View on Map →

Vitosha Mountain - Dragalevtsi

📏 12 km ⏱️ 1.5-2 hours Challenging

Mountain cycling with elevation gain. Beautiful forest roads, challenging climbs. For experienced cyclists with good diabetes control.

View on Map →

Studentski Grad - Mladost

📏 8 km ⏱️ 40-50 min Easy

Flat urban route through residential areas with bike lanes. Parks, shops, and pharmacies along the way.

View on Map →

Simeonovo to Aleko (Vitosha)

📏 18 km ⏱️ 2-3 hours Challenging

Epic mountain climb to Aleko ski resort. Significant elevation gain, requires excellent fitness and diabetes management.

View on Map →

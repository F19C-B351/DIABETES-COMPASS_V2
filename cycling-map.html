🗺️ Cycling Routes for Diabetics

Explore safe, scenic cycling routes in Sofia and beyond. These routes are selected with diabetics in mind—featuring rest stops, accessible locations, and varying difficulty levels. Always carry your glucose supplies and ride safely!

🚴 Popular Cycling Routes

South Park (Yuzhen Park)

📏 5-8 km loop ⏱️ 30-45 min Easy

Flat, paved cycling paths through Sofia's largest park. Multiple rest areas, water fountains, and cafes. Perfect for beginners.

💡 Route Planning Tips for Diabetics

🏪 Know Rest Stops

Plan routes near shops, cafes, or pharmacies where you can get carbs or supplies if needed.

📱 Download Offline Maps

Save routes offline in case you need to find help or a rest stop without cell service.

🔄 Plan Loop Routes

Loop routes mean you're never too far from your starting point if you need to cut the ride short.

📊 Match Difficulty to Glucose

Start with easier routes and gradually increase difficulty as you learn how cycling affects your blood sugar.

👥 Share Your Route

Tell someone your planned route and expected return time. Use GPS tracking apps.

🌡️ Check Weather

Extreme heat or cold affects blood sugar. Avoid cycling in harsh conditions.
